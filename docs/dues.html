Wake'n'Make has a sliding-scale fee structure to encourage folks of all financial ability to participate. There are three schedules of dues, based on either day-use (weekday vs. weekend) or per-month usage. The fee schedule is given in the table below.

Fee Schedule Minimum Fee Maximum Fee
Monthly $50 $200
Daily (weekday) $10 $20
Daily (weekend) $15 $25

Wake'n'Make shop accepts payments via cash or Venmo (@wakenmake). Please add a note to your Venmo payment with the purpose of the payment (e.g., "Monthly Dues", "Daily Dues", etc.).
